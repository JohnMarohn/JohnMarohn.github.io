Congratulations Dr. Tirmzi

Congratulations to Ali Tirmzi for successfully defending his PhD thesis:

Tirmzi, A. M.“Photoconductivity Dynamics in Lead Halide Perovskites Probed by Scanning Probe Microscopy”, Cornell University, 2020 (172 pages, 46.5 MB).

Ali will shortly begin work as a TD Etch Module Engineer at Intel Corporation (Portland, OR). Follow Ali on LinkedIn here. Ali’s thesis work was funded by the U.S. National Science Foundation.

Celebrating Ali’s Tirmzi’s successful PhD defense with champaign. (L to R) Marohn group members Michael Boucher, Dr. Ali Tirmzi, Rachael Cohn, and Virginia McGhee.
