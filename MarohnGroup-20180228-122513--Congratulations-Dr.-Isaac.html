Congratulations Dr. Isaac

Congratulations to Corinne Issac for successfully defending her PhD thesis this month:

Isaac, C. E.“Harnessing Electron Spin Labels for Single Molecule Magnetic Resonance Imaging”, Cornell University, 2018 (331 pages, 144 MB).

Corinne will start a new job soon as a Chemical and Surface Measurement Scientist at Corning Incorporated (Corning, NY). Follow Corinne on LinkedIn here.

Dr. Isaac’s thesis work was funded by the U.S. National Science Foundation and the U.S. Army Research Office.
